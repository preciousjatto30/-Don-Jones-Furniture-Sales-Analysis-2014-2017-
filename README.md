# -Don-Jones-Furniture-Sales-Analysis-2014-2017-

This project analyzes the Don-Jones Furniture sales performance from 2014 to 2017 using an interactive dashboard. The analysis focuses on key business metrics such as total sales, profit, product performance, geographic distribution, shipping . The findings provide data-driven  to support better business decisions and improve overall profitability.

📊 Project Overview
This project presents a comprehensive sales performance analysis of the Don-Jones Furniture dataset from 2014 to 2017. The goal of the analysis is to extract actionable insights from sales data and provide strategic recommendations that can support better business decision-making.
Using an interactive dashboard, the analysis explores sales trends, profitability, geographic distribution, product performance, shipping patterns, and customer segment profitability.
The dashboard consolidates key metrics and visual insights to help stakeholders quickly understand what drives revenue, where opportunities exist, and which areas require improvement.
🎯 Aim

To analyze furniture sales performance between 2014 and 2017 and uncover insights that can help improve revenue growth, profitability, and operational efficiency.

📌 Objectives
Analyze overall sales and profit performance.
Identify seasonal sales patterns and peak months.
Evaluate product category contribution to revenue.
Examine geographic sales distribution across states and cities.
Analyze shipping mode utilization.
Assess order duration patterns.
Evaluate profitability across customer segments.
Identify risks, opportunities, and operational challenges.

⚠️ Key Business Challenges

💡 Strategic Recommendations
Improve pricing strategy for high-demand products.
Expand sales efforts in underperforming regions.
Diversify product revenue beyond chairs.
